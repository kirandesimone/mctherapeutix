About

Mario Castillo is a Pennsylvania licensed Massage Therapist who believes in the mind, body, and spirit connection. His core value expresses the sense that taking care of our bodies makes us happier as well as aides in our physical health. Therefore, we should listen to our bodies so we can reach a state of balance and inner harmony.

Mario is a graduate of The Cortiva Institute where he was trained and certified. He specializes in massage modalities including Swedish, deep tissue, sports, craniosacral, and stretching.

Mario has over 8-years’ experience working with clients of all backgrounds and strongly believes in the power of positive thinking, as well as work/life integration. Mario regularly develops internal wellness campaigns to assist colleagues with reaching productive internal and external optimum health potential. Mario’s other interests include Calisthenics, Arial Yoga, and spending time in nature.
